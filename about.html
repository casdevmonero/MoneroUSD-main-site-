MoneroUSD Details

How MoneroUSD Maintains the Peg

Reserve Backing

BTC and XMR held in on-chain reserve addresses. Ratio must exceed 150% of all minted USDm.

Burn & Mint

USDm is minted when crypto is deposited, and burned (sent to provably-unspendable address) when redeemed for crypto.

Fee Revenue

Swap fees: 80% reserves, 15% yield pool, 5% operations. Lending interest: 50% reserves, 40% yield, 10% ops.

FCMP++ vs Ring Signatures

Monero: Ring Signatures

Each transaction includes a ring of 16 decoy outputs. The anonymity set is limited to ring size.

MoneroUSD: FCMP++

Full-Chain Membership Proofs prove a spent output belongs to the entire UTXO set. The anonymity set is the full blockchain.
