How MoneroUSD Maintains the Peg

Reserve Backing

BTC and XMR held in protocol reserve wallets — these wallets ARE the treasury. Total reserve value must exceed 150% of all minted USDm at all times.

1.5:1 Swap Ratio

Deposit $150 in BTC or XMR → receive 100 USDm. Every swap entry instantly grows the reserve above 150%. USDm can always be redeemed for the underlying crypto.

Mining Gate

Auto-mining is gated by reserve health. Block rewards only issue when reserve ≥ 150% of supply. Mining halts automatically if the ratio would drop below the floor.

Fee Revenue

Swap fees (0.5%): 80% reserves, 15% yield pool, 5% ops. Lending interest: 40% yield pool, remainder to reserves. Staking yield only paid from accumulated loan interest — never touches reserves.

FCMP++ vs Ring Signatures

Monero: Ring Signatures

Each transaction includes a ring of 16 decoy outputs. The anonymity set is limited to ring size.

MoneroUSD: FCMP++

Full-Chain Membership Proofs prove a spent output belongs to the entire UTXO set. The anonymity set is the full blockchain.
